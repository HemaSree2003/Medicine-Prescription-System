Drugs for Headache:

    Age-Below 5 years

  • Fioricet-5mg
  • Ibuprofen-10mg

    • Age-Between 5-18 years

  • Acetaminophen-10mg
  • Naproxen-20mg

    • Age-Above 18 years

  • provanol-50mg
  • Advil-100mg

Note:

Use these medicines twice a day after having food for three days or less, otherwise consult doctor.

Food to be taken:

  • Hydration - Water:
    • Dehydration can contribute to headaches, so it's crucial to stay well-hydrated. Drink plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Caffine:
    • In moderate amounts, caffeine can have a headache-relieving effect by constricting blood vessels. However, excessive caffeine intake can lead to withdrawal headaches, so it's essential to maintain a balanced consumption.
  • Magnesium-Rich Foods:
    • Magnesium may help relax blood vessels and alleviate headaches. Include magnesium-rich foods like spinach, almonds, and avocados in your diet.
  • Ginger:
    • Known for its anti-inflammatory properties, ginger may help reduce headache symptoms. Consider ginger tea or adding fresh ginger to your meals.
  • Dark Leafy Greens:
    • Foods like kale and spinach contain various vitamins and minerals, including B vitamins, which can contribute to overall headache relief.
