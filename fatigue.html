Drugs for Fatigue:

    Age-Below 5 years

  • Amitriptyline-5mg
  • Nortriptyline-10mg

    • Age-Between 5-18 years

  • Trazodone-10mg
  • Doxepin-20mg

    • Age-Above 18 years

  • Elavil-100mg
  • Mirazapine-50mg

Note:

Use these medicines twice a day after having food for three days or less, otherwise consult doctor.

Food to be taken:

  • Complex Carbohydrates:
    • Whole grains like oats, quinoa, and brown rice release energy slowly, helping to maintain stable blood sugar levels and prevent energy crashes.
  • Lean Proteins:
    • Foods rich in lean protein, such as chicken, fish, tofu, and legumes, can provide a steady release of energy and support muscle function.
  • Fruits and Vegetables:
    • Packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, fruits and vegetables contribute to overall health and can provide a natural energy boost.
  • Nuts and Seeds:
    • Almonds, walnuts, chia seeds, and flaxseeds are good sources of healthy fats, fiber, and protein, offering a sustained source of energy.
  • Hydrating Foods:
    • Staying well-hydrated is crucial for combating fatigue. Include water-rich foods like watermelon, cucumber, and celery in your diet.
