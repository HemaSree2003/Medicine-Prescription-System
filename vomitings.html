Drugs for Vomitings:

    Age-Below 5 years

  • Arladram-5mg
  • Calm-x-10mg

    • Age-Between 5-18 years

  • di-men-10mg
  • di-nate-20mg

    • Age-Above 18 years

  • dimenest-100mg
  • dimenhydrinate-100mg

Note:

Use these medicines twice a day after having food for three days or less, otherwise consult doctor.

Food to be taken:

  • Clear Broth:
    • Clear vegetable or chicken broth provides hydration and essential nutrients without being too heavy on the stomach.
  • Crackers or Toast:
    • Plain crackers or toast can help absorb stomach acid and provide a bland source of carbohydrates.
  • Bananas:
    • Bananas are gentle on the stomach and provide potassium, which can be beneficial during vomiting.
  • Applesauce:
    • Unsweetened applesauce is easy to digest and can provide a source of energy.
  • Electrolyte Drinks:
    • Oral rehydration solutions or sports drinks can help replace lost electrolytes and fluids during vomiting.
