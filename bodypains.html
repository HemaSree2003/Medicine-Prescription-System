Drugs for Body pains:

    Age-Below 5 years

  • Ibuprofen-5mg
  • Acetaminophen-10mg

    • Age-Between 5-18 years

  • Naproxen-10mg
  • Diclofenac-20mg

    • Age-Above 18 years

  • Zerodol-100mg
  • Tylenol-100mg

Note:

Use these medicines twice a day after having food for three days or less, otherwise consult doctor.

Food to be taken:

  • Fatty Fish:
    • Rich in omega-3 fatty acids, fish like salmon and mackerel have anti-inflammatory effects that may help alleviate body pains.
  • Turmeric:
    • Contains curcumin, a compound with powerful anti-inflammatory properties. Adding turmeric to dishes or consuming turmeric tea can be beneficial.
  • Leafy Greens:
    • Vegetables like kale, spinach, and Swiss chard are high in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, which can contribute to overall health and reduce inflammation.
  • Berries:
    • Blueberries, strawberries, and raspberries are packed with antioxidants, which may help reduce inflammation and provide pain relief.
  • Nuts and Seeds:
    • Almonds, walnuts, and flaxseeds are good sources of omega-3 fatty acids and antioxidants, contributing to an anti-inflammatory diet.
