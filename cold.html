Drugs for Cold:

    Age-Below 5 years

  • Benadryl-10mg
  • Diphenhydramine-20mg

    • Age-Between 5-18 years

  • Chlorpheniramine-50mg
  • Chest and cold-100mg

    • Age-Above 18 years

  • Promethazine-100mg
  • Citrazin-100mg

Note:

Use these medicines twice a day after having food for three days or less, otherwise consult doctor.

Food to be taken:

  • Soups:
    • Warm and soothing,soup can help relieve congestion and provide hydration.
  • Citrus Fruits:
    • Oranges, lemons, and grapefruits are rich in vitamin C, which can support the immune system.
  • Hot Tea with Honey:
    • Warm tea, especially with honey, can soothe a sore throat and provide comfort.
  • Garlic:
    • Known for its antibacterial properties, garlic can support the immune system. Add it to soups or meals.
  • Spicy Foods:
    • Foods with spices like ginger and chili can help clear nasal congestion and provide relief.
