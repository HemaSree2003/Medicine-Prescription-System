Drugs for Cough:

    Age-Below 5 years

  • Benzonatate-10mg
  • Mucinex-10mg

    • Age-Between 5-18 years

  • Guaifenesin-20mg
  • Benadryl-100mg

    • Age-Above 18 years

  • Cheratussin AC-100mg
  • Promethazine DM-100mg

Note:

Use these medicines twice a day after having food for three days or less, otherwise consult doctor.

Food to be taken:

  • Broth-based Soups:
    • Provide hydration and essential nutrients.
  • Herbal Teas:
    • Ginger, peppermint, and chamomile can soothe the throat.
  • Honey and Warm Water:
    • Mix for a comforting, throat-soothing drink.
  • Soft Fruits:
    • Opt for easy-to-eat fruits like bananas and melons.
  • Hydrating Smoothies:
    • Blend fruits with yogurt for a nutritious and easy-to-swallow option.
