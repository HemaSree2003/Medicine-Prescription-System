Drugs for Acidity:

    Age-Below 5 years

  • Prevacid-10mg
  • Nexium-5mg

    • Age-Between 5-18 years

  • Prilosec OTC-10mg
  • Zegerid OTC-20mg

    • Age-Above 18 years

  • omeprazole-40mg
  • pantoprazole-20mg

Note:

Use these medicines twice a day after having food for three days or less, otherwise consult doctor.

Food to be taken:
  • Oatmeal:
    • A low-acid whole grain that can help absorb excess stomach acid.
  • Bananas:
    • Non-citrus fruits like bananas are generally easy on the stomach and can help neutralize acidity.
  • Ginger:
    • Known for its digestive properties, ginger can help soothe the digestive tract and reduce acidity.
  • Melons:
    • Watermelon and cantaloupe are low-acid fruits that can be gentle on the stomach.
  • Green Vegetables:
    • Non-acidic vegetables such as broccoli, spinach, and green beans are good choices.
