Drugs for Swelling:

    Age-Below 5 years

  • Aspirin-5mg
  • Naproxen-10mg

    • Age-Between 5-18 years

  • Ibuprofen-50mg
  • Diclofenac-100mg

    • Age-Above 18 years

  • Advil-100mg
  • Celecoxib-100mg

Note:

Use these medicines twice a day after having food for three days or less, otherwise consult doctor.

Food to be taken:

  • Fatty Fish:
    • Rich in omega-3 fatty acids, fish like salmon and mackerel have anti-inflammatory effects that may help reduce swelling.
  • Turmeric:
    • Contains curcumin, a natural anti-inflammatory compound. Incorporate turmeric into your diet through curries, soups, or turmeric tea.
  • Berries:
    • Blueberries, strawberries, and raspberries are high in antioxidants, which have anti-inflammatory properties that may help combat swelling.
  • Leafy Greens:
    • Kale, spinach, and other leafy greens are rich in vitamins and minerals that can contribute to overall health and potentially reduce inflammation.
  • Ginger:
    • Known for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, ginger can be added to meals or consumed as ginger tea to help with swelling.
